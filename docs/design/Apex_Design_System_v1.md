1. Design Philosophy
Design Goals
Minimal, not empty
Premium, not flashy
Fast, not distracting
Professional, not playful
AI-first
Accessibility-first
Consistency over creativity
Motion with purpose
Design Principles
Principle 1

Information First

UI exists to help users understand information faster.

Principle 2

Whitespace is a Feature

Don't fill every gap.

Whitespace improves comprehension.

Principle 3

One Primary Action

Every screen has one obvious CTA.

Principle 4

Motion Explains

Animation should communicate state.

Never animate for decoration.

Principle 5

Everything Feels Alive

Buttons

Cards

Inputs

Navigation

Everything responds subtly.
